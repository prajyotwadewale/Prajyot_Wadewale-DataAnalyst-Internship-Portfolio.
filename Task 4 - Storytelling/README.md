## 🧠 Approach

### 3️⃣ Hypothesis Testing

* **H₀ (Null Hypothesis):** No difference in revenue across months

## 📊 Key Insights

* Revenue increases in Q4 (seasonal trend)
* UK is the highest revenue-generating country
* Top products drive majority of sales
* Most customers are low-value

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Increase marketing during Q4
* Focus on UK market
* Promote top products
* Improve customer retention
